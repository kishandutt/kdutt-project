STAFF PICKS

Here's what we've been jamming to on repeat!

by SARAH REYNOLDS

At Fizzle, our passionate team has always been dedicated to exploring diverse musical landscapes and rediscovering the cherished favorites that have left an indelible mark on our hearts. This time, we're thrilled to share the albums that hold a special place in our souls, traversing the rich terrains of alternative rock, instrumental innovation, punk legends, and contemporary jazz.

Wilco's albums, "Yankee Hotel Foxtrot" and "A Ghost Is Born," are our constant companions. They captivate us with their intricate lyricism and sonic diversity, showcasing Wilco's ever-willingness to push boundaries and redefine the genre. These albums are more than favorites; they are our musical confidants.

Trans Am's creations, such as "Futureworld" and "Red Line," electrify our senses with their futuristic instrumentals. Repetitive melodies interlaced with bursts of unforgettable drumming will leave you breathless. It will take you for a ride!

In the early hardcore realm, the Minutemen's "Double Nickels on the Dime" stands as a legendary masterpiece, and for us, it is a cherished favorite. This album is not merely a call to action; it's a snapshot of punk's spirit, where every note and lyric resonates with raw intensity and political insight. The Minutemen's ability to blend punk, funk, and spoken word is the hallmark of their distinctive sound, and their influence on the punk scene is immeasurable. This next clip is an all-time favorite of ours.

Hiromi's albums, "Brain" and "Spiral," are not just favorites; they are our personal gateways to contemporary jazz excellence. Her fusion of virtuosic piano compositions and dynamic arrangements speaks to our very souls. We also treasure her collaborations with Simon Phillips and Anthony Jackson in the Hiromi Trio Project, which take us on an even more profound journey where technical prowess meets emotional depth.

Everyone on staff had some input in the choices made for this list. Each album mentioned has made an indelible mark on our hearts, pushing the boundaries of creativity and contributing to the diverse tapestry of sound. As we continue to explore the ever-expanding world of music, we invite you to join us in celebrating these extraordinary artists and their contributions to our lives. Music is more than an adventure; it's a lifelong love story worth savoring.

About the Author
